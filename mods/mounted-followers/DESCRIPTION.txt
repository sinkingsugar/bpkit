MOUNTED FOLLOWERS
=================

By Crom, why is your bodyguard JOGGING?

You thunder across the Exiled Lands on your warhorse while your faithful
thrall sprints behind you like a peasant chasing a runaway cart. Pathetic.
You are a warlord. Warlords ride with a RETINUE.

Mounted Followers puts every humanoid follower in a saddle of their own the
moment you mount up. Your warband rides when you ride. Dismount, and they
swing down beside you, ready to spill blood on foot.

HOW IT WORKS
- Bring your followers AND enough spare (tamed, following) horses.
- Mount up. Every humanoid follower claims their own horse and saddles up.
- Spare horses trail behind you in a staggered line - a column of riders,
  not a horse-pile.
- Dismount and the whole retinue dismounts with you.

FEATURES
- One horse per follower - no clown-stacking.
- Raises your Mount follower cap (+5 by default) so the warband actually
  fits - and touches ONLY the Mount group, so it won't fight thrall-cap
  mods like Better Thralls.
- Set the retinue size yourself: admins (and solo players, who are their
  own admin) run `dc MFHorses N` to change the extra mounted slots per
  player. Takes effect live, no restart, and it sticks across reloads.
- Works in single-player AND multiplayer.
- Pure logic mod: no map edits, no asset overrides, no replaced files.
  Stacks peacefully with your other 40 mods.

THE HONEST CORNER
- Followers will not fight from the saddle - mounted combat is hardcoded
  player-only in Conan's engine. Dismount to brawl; your crew unsaddles
  with you. Working as intended: even Conan got off his horse to kill.
- Riders hold a seated pose while mounted (they are passengers, not
  jockeys). The horses do the running.
- On relog everything respawns clean - worst case your thralls are
  standing, not broken.
- Multiplayer: EVERY player's retinue rides - each player is tracked
  server-side, and a rider whose owner logs out mid-ride is calmly put
  back on their feet. Listen servers are the tested path; dedicated-server
  reports are especially welcome.

EXPERIMENTAL - RIDE AT THE FRONT OF THE CHARGE
This is an early release. It is tested in single-player and on a listen
server, but the Exiled Lands are vast and server setups are many. PLEASE
report what you find - especially multiplayer quirks (dedicated servers,
high follower counts, mod conflicts): drop a comment here, or open an
issue at https://github.com/sinkingsugar/bpkit. Every report makes the
warband ride harder.
